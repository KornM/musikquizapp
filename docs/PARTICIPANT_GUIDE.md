## Getting Started

### What You Need

- **Device**: Smartphone, tablet, or computer
- **Internet**: Stable internet connection
- **Browser**: Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- **Audio**: Working speakers or headphones

### How It Works

1. **Register**: Create your profile once
2. **Join**: Scan QR code or click link to join a quiz
3. **Listen**: Hear audio clips of songs
4. **Answer**: Choose the correct answer from 4 options
5. **Score**: Earn points for correct answers
6. **Win**: Top the scoreboard!

1. During a quiz, click the **profile icon** in the top right
2. Or visit the profile page directly
3. You'll see:

## Playing the Quiz

### Quiz Interface

When the quiz starts, you'll see:
- **Round Number**: Current round (e.g., "Round 1 of 10")
- **Audio Player**: Plays the song clip
- **Answer Options**: Four buttons with possible answers
- **Timer**: Countdown showing time remaining (if enabled)
- **Your Score**: Your current total points

### Answering Questions

1. **Listen to Audio**
   - Audio plays automatically when the round starts
   - Listen carefully to the song clip
   - You can replay by clicking the play button

2. **Choose Your Answer**
   - Four answer options are displayed
   - Tap or click the answer you think is correct
   - Your selection is highlighted

3. **Submit Answer**
   - Click **"Submit"** to confirm your answer
   - Or your answer auto-submits when time runs out

4. **Wait for Results**
   - After submitting, you'll see if you were correct
   - Points are awarded for correct answers
   - Wait for the next round to start

### Scoring System

- **Correct Answer**: Earn points (typically 100 points)
- **Fast Answer**: Bonus points for quick responses (if enabled)
- **Wrong Answer**: No points, but no penalty
- **No Answer**: No points if you don't submit in time

**Example Scoring:**
```
Correct answer: +100 points
Fast answer bonus: +20 points
Total: 120 points
```

### Between Rounds

Between rounds, you'll see:
- **Your Answer**: What you selected
- **Correct Answer**: The right answer highlighted
- **Points Earned**: Points for that round
- **Current Rank**: Your position on the scoreboard
- **Next Round**: Countdown to next round

## Tips for Success

### Before the Quiz

1. **Test Your Device**: Ensure audio works properly
2. **Good Connection**: Use stable Wi-Fi or strong cellular signal
3. **Charge Battery**: Make sure your device is charged
4. **Quiet Environment**: Find a quiet place to hear audio clearly
5. **Headphones**: Use headphones for better audio quality

### During the Quiz

1. **Listen Carefully**: Pay attention to the entire audio clip
2. **Don't Rush**: Take time to think before answering
3. **Trust Your Instinct**: Your first guess is often correct
4. **Stay Focused**: Avoid distractions during the quiz
5. **Have Fun**: Remember, it's about enjoying music!

### Strategy Tips

1. **Eliminate Wrong Answers**: Rule out obviously wrong options
2. **Listen for Clues**: Pay attention to vocals, instruments, style
3. **Use Context**: Consider the quiz theme (e.g., "80s Music")
4. **Learn from Mistakes**: Note correct answers for future reference
5. **Stay Positive**: Don't get discouraged by wrong answers

### Audio Recognition Tips

1. **Listen to Intro**: Opening notes are often distinctive
2. **Identify Vocals**: Recognize the singer's voice
3. **Note Instruments**: Unique instruments can be clues
4. **Remember Lyrics**: Familiar lyrics help identify songs
5. **Feel the Beat**: Rhythm and tempo are identifying features

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Register

**Problem**: Registration page won't load
**Solutions**:
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Ask organizer for a new link

### Audio Not Playing

**Problem**: Can't hear the audio clips
**Solutions**:
- Check device volume is turned up
- Unmute your device
- Check browser permissions for audio
- Try using headphones
- Refresh the page
- Try a different browser

### Can't Submit Answer

**Problem**: Submit button doesn't work
**Solutions**:
- Make sure you've selected an answer
- Check internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Contact quiz organizer

### Disconnected During Quiz

**Problem**: Lost connection during quiz
**Solutions**:
- Reconnect to internet
- Refresh the page
- Your progress is saved
- You can rejoin where you left off

### Wrong Name or Avatar

**Problem**: Want to change profile
**Solutions**:
- Click profile icon
- Click "Edit Profile"
- Update information
- Save changes
- Changes apply to future quizzes

### Scoreboard Not Updating

**Problem**: Scoreboard shows old scores
**Solutions**:
- Pull down to refresh (mobile)
- Click refresh button
- Wait a few seconds for auto-update
- Check internet connection

### Device Issues

**Problem**: Quiz not working on device
**Solutions**:
- Update your browser
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Restart your device
- Try a different device

## Frequently Asked Questions

### General Questions

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: No traditional account needed. Just register with a name and avatar when you join your first quiz.

**Q: Can I use the same profile for multiple quizzes?**
A: Yes! Your profile is saved and automatically used for all quizzes in the same organization.

**Q: Can I change my name after registering?**
A: Yes, you can edit your profile at any time. Changes apply to future quizzes.

**Q: Is my information private?**
A: Only your name and avatar are visible to others. No personal information is shared.

**Q: Can I participate from multiple devices?**
A: You can, but you'll need to register on each device. Your profile is device-specific.

### Quiz Questions

**Q: How many rounds are in a quiz?**
A: It varies by quiz. Typically 10-30 rounds. The organizer decides.

**Q: How much time do I have to answer?**
A: Time limits vary by quiz. Usually 15-30 seconds per question.

**Q: Can I change my answer after submitting?**
A: No, answers are final once submitted.

**Q: What happens if I don't answer in time?**
A: You receive no points for that round, but can continue with the next round.

**Q: Can I replay the audio?**
A: Yes, click the play button to hear the clip again (if time permits).

### Scoring Questions

**Q: How are points calculated?**
A: Correct answers earn points. Some quizzes award bonus points for fast answers.

**Q: Do I lose points for wrong answers?**
A: No, there's no penalty for incorrect answers.

**Q: How is the winner determined?**
A: The participant with the highest total points wins.

**Q: What happens in case of a tie?**
A: Ties are typically broken by fastest average response time.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions).

**Q: Do I need to install an app?**
A: No, the quiz works in your web browser. No app installation needed.

**Q: Does it work on mobile?**
A: Yes, fully optimized for smartphones and tablets.

**Q: What if my internet is slow?**
A: The quiz works on slower connections, but audio may take longer to load.

**Q: Can I participate offline?**
A: No, an internet connection is required throughout the quiz.
